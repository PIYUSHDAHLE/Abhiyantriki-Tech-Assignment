Investor Relations

Overview

Investor Relations (IR) function has seen a tremendous shift in its relevance and role with increased investor engagement, enhanced shareholder participation, and emergence of proxy advisory firms; all on the back of an enhanced focus on corporate governance. In line with this shift, the role of the Investor Relations Officer (IRO) has also undergone a change.

Not only is the IRO responsible for the correct messaging to the investors and the financial community at large, he/she is also increasingly expected to provide industry insights due to his/her outward-facing role and access to the investors.

These developments demand that the IROs act as the internal counsel for the Csuite executives at their respective companies further increasing their responsibilities and broadening their profile. The IR function has the potential to significantly enhance a company’s profile in the financial markets if backed by the right business performance and intent. A highly effective IR function can truly operate as the steward of optimum value for all stakeholders.

Program Overview

The Bootcamp in Investor Relations (IR) in collaboration with KPMG in India is a comprehensive program designed to provide participants with detailed insights into the multifaceted functions of Investor Relations (IR).


Operating at the intersection of communication, research, analysis, and compliance, IR team plays a pivotal role in aligning corporate strategies with the expectations of investors and other key stakeholders.

This bootcamp, facilitated by seasoned professionals from KPMG India, will cover all aspects of IR team responsibilities, from gathering and analyzing financial and operational data to creating effective disclosures and following regulatory standards.


Participants will gain insights into critical areas of IR, including equity narratives, regulatory disclosures, ESG integration, IR tools, and crisis and stakeholder management. The program focuses on practical insights through discussions based on real-life examples, challenges, and a capstone project submission.

Capstone Project

Participants to work in teams to conduct an Investor Relations Health Check for a selected listed corporate entity - IR strategy, IR Collaterals, IR Communications etc.

The assignment will enable participants to assess their understanding of the applicability of all the topics covered during the bootcamp.
